Frequent breaker trips, warm breaker covers, or flickering lights can point to an overloaded or aging panel. Homes adding EV chargers, hot tubs, or heat pumps often need more capacity.

An upgrade improves safety, gives room for dedicated circuits, and can prepare your home for future electrification. We install code‑compliant panels with whole‑home surge protection and clear labeling.
